Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduPath free to use?

Yes! EduPath is completely free for all students. Our basic assessment, college directory, and career guidance are available at no cost.

How accurate is the career assessment?

Our assessment is designed by educational psychologists and validated with thousands of student outcomes. It provides reliable guidance based on proven aptitude and interest matching methods.

Which states and colleges are covered?

We cover government colleges across all Indian states and union territories, with detailed information on courses, eligibility, and admission processes.

Can parents access my results?

Your assessment results are private by default. You can choose to share them with parents or counselors through our secure sharing features.
